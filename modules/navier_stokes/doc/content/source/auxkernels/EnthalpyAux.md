# EnthalpyAux

!syntax description /AuxKernels/EnthalpyAux

This AuxKernel computes the specific enthalpy $h$ of the fluid from the total energy $\rho e$
and the pressure $P$.

\begin{equation}
h = \dfrac{\rho e + P}{\rho}
\end{equation}
