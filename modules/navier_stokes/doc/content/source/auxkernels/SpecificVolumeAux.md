# SpecificVolumeAux

!syntax description /AuxKernels/SpecificVolumeAux

This auxkernel computes the specific volume $v$ of the fluid.

\begin{equation}
v = \dfrac{\alpha * A}{\rho}
\end{equation}

with $\alpha$ the volume fraction, $A$ the area and $\rho$ the density.
